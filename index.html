This calculator takes a collection of samples of the relative abundance of Benthic MacroInvertebrates in a river and calculates the average population in that river. Abundance of Benthic MacroInvertebrates is a key measure of the health of the water in a river. Thic calculator requires a number of Benthic MacroInvertebrate abundance values (decimal values between 1 and 10) and then calculates the average health of the river based on these values.
